About

The short version.

Johnathan is a product manager with 6+ years of experience building data and AI-powered products in complex, regulated environments. He builds things that work: an AI app generating $300K per year in value, a lead-gen tool driving $1.5M in pipeline, an analytics platform that saw 800% usage growth. When he's not shipping product, he's vibe-coding internal tools with Cursor and Claude Code.
